Cypermon – Support

If you need help with the Cypermon mobile game, you can reach us using the support information below.

Contact Support

Email: cypermon.game@gmail.com
Developer: Ali Al-Sultan

Please include as much detail as possible when you contact us, such as:

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 13, iPad, etc.)
  • Your iOS version
  • The version of Cypermon you are using
  • A short description of the problem
  • Screenshots if available

Common Issues

1. I cannot log in

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and that you are using the latest version of the app from the App Store. If the issue continues, contact us with your account email.

2. The game crashes or freezes

Restart your device and try again. If the problem continues, please tell us your device model and when exactly the crash happens (for example: during loading, in a specific screen, etc.).

3. My progress is missing

If you used an account or login method, please log in again using the same account. If your progress is still missing, contact us with your account email and any details you remember.
